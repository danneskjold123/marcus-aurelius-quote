About

This single-page application provides randomized quotes from Marcus Aurelius' Meditations. All quotes are taken from public domain translations and embedded directly in the page so the generator works entirely offline after saving index.html.

Use the "New Quote" button or reload the page to receive a fresh passage of Stoic insight for reflection. The design is intentionally minimalist to keep attention on the wisdom.

Privacy Policy

This static page does not collect personal data. No analytics, no external resources, and no third-party trackers are included by default. If you add any scripts (for ads or analytics), review their privacy practices before publishing.

The app uses your browser's local capabilities only (copy/share features are client-side). If you deploy ads, include your own disclosures as required by applicable policies (for example, AdSense requires an About and Privacy page).
